About Me


Stacy McQuerrey

Experienced Wedding and Fantasy Photographer
Galion, Ohio

Hello, I'm Stacy!
I'm a photography enthusiast that resides in Galion, Ohio - near Mansfield. I photograph Weddings, engagements, portraits, and model all across Ohio.


Experience:

Wedding Photography, Fairytale/Fantasy, Boudoir, Senior Portraits, Engagements, Maternity, Family Portraits and Animals.


Passions:

Many of my passions go hand and hand with my love of photography. In my spare time, I enjoy creating beautiful, flowing or flowered dresses from ones I picked from second hand shops. I often put together the setting placement as well. I enjoy looking through Pinterest in search of my next hair inspiration or some make-up ideas. I spend time on YouTube trying to perfect hair and make-up techniques for my next shoot.


Overview:

I have pursued a career as an independent photographer for well over 10 years. During my photography journey, I have found that photographing people cannot be compared to any other type of photography. To capture the raw beauty behind the eyes in what I am after. I have always been fascinated with the models in advertisements contained within magazines such as 'Vogue' and how the image can pursuade people to buy the product.
